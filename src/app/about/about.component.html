How to use GetResume.io

To get your perfect free resume you need to

  1. Enter the information you want in the all fields of the form.
  2. Upload your photo.
  3. Click on button "Preview".

Afterwards, the new page with your ready resume will be opened.

How to save a resume as a PDF file ?

Web browser's menu bar:

  1. Choose File > Print.
  2. Click the Save as PDF.
  3. Unchecked "Headers and footers" options if it is needed.
  4. Choose a name and location for the PDF file. Enter the information you want in the Title, Author and Keywords fields.
